Resources for healthcare providers

We arranged resources in two ways. One is 5M’s that assist clinicians to focus on most important topics for people coming with cognitive impairment. The clinicians who adapted this model found it very helpful in providing the care.

Second way is based on literature review, where we have found “unmet needs” of health professionals, who care for dementia patients/caregivers and wanted to have more information on specific topics to effectively manage the population with the cognitive impairment.
